#For the best experience don't use the apk and instead install the app like Ionescu Andrei did in the demo or look at the short tutorial below.

##Short tutorial

###iOS
1. Open safari -> Type in the navbar idcrew.shop or [click here](https://idcrew.shop) 
![1](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/ad2858da-13c3-4f17-a893-1b6507835885)
2. After you click enter, the website with the login page will appear;
3. Now click on the share button from the bottom of the page;
3. ![2](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/bb0d8797-30ee-401b-ae6a-f2e6f5ea131e)

4. After you click on share, search for the option "ADD TO HOME SCREEN" and select it;
![3](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/e906848f-1305-48ae-a70f-9f50e6ed9bd0)
5. Then, wait a few seconds until the app image will show up and hit "ADD" in the top right corner;
6. ![4](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/2261eacd-625e-4242-847a-457d9ae92eca)
7. JFS now should appear on your home page.
![5](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/44424c42-2b42-403b-aa5d-9f292e1e78ec)

###ANDROID

1. Open Chrome -> Type in the navbar idcrew.shop or [click here](https://idcrew.shop) 
2. After you click enter, the website with the login page will appear;
4. Now click on the 3 dots from the top right of the page;
5. After you click on the 3 dots, search for the option "INSTALL" and click on it;
6. Then, wait a few seconds until the app will be installed, the process will appear in the notifications pannel of your phone;
7. After the instalation is done, the app will apear on the home page, and is ready to use it.
