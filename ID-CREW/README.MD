# For the best experience don't use the apk and instead install the app like Ionescu Andrei did in the demo or take a look at the short tutorial below.

## Short tutorial

### iOS
1. Open safari -> Type in the navbar idcrew.shop or [click here](https://idcrew.shop) 
![1](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/ad2858da-13c3-4f17-a893-1b6507835885)
2. After you click enter, the website with the login page will appear;
3. Now click on the share button from the bottom of the page;
![2](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/bb0d8797-30ee-401b-ae6a-f2e6f5ea131e)
4. After you click on share, search for the option "ADD TO HOME SCREEN" and select it;
![3](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/e906848f-1305-48ae-a70f-9f50e6ed9bd0)
5. Then, wait a few seconds until the app image will show up and hit "ADD" in the top right corner;
![4](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/2261eacd-625e-4242-847a-457d9ae92eca)
6. JFS now should appear on your home page.
![5](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/44424c42-2b42-403b-aa5d-9f292e1e78ec)

### ANDROID

1. Open Chrome -> Type in the navbar idcrew.shop or [click here](https://idcrew.shop) 
![1](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/ea9dff11-14d9-4a3e-a1a8-7569fdf50763)
2. After you click enter, the website with the login page will appear;
3. Now click on the 3 dots from the top right of the page;
![2](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/2e497afd-0f3d-43ff-8cf1-55fe083795fd)
4 After you click on the 3 dots, search for the option "INSTALL" and click on it;
![3](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/e7576195-1f25-447f-bee2-78d2adec9036)
5.Then, wait a few seconds until the app will be installed, the process will appear in the notifications pannel of your phone;
![4](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/1954471f-d732-4082-bfbd-3610a7d00659)
6. After the instalation is done, the app will apear on the home page, and is ready to use it.
![5](https://github.com/Alexandrudiun/ID-CREW/assets/129840688/a633bc44-637c-4a3d-b546-55d120a60bdd)
